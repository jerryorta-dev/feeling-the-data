Flux Software Development Knowledge Brain Dependency Injector (FSDKBDI).

Test your knowledge on various software technologies.

There are two "modes" to take a quiz:

Timed

  • Choose the max number of questions per quiz.
  • Time limit is set to 30 seconds per quiz.

Review

  • Each answer is linked to documentation for review.
  • There is no time limit per question.
  • You get immediate feedback upon answering each question.
  • You may navigate forward or back through a quiz.
  • Each answer is linked to it's documentation.
